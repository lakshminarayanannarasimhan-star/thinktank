# Causal Systems Diagnostics

## Systems Thinking Lab

**Author:** Lakshmi Narayanan Narasimhan

Causal Systems Diagnostics is a framework for understanding complex systems through **causal mechanisms, system archetypes, and structural analysis**.

The framework is being developed through the **Elite Think Tank Mastery Program**, a long-term cognitive training initiative focused on building expertise in systems thinking and strategic reasoning.

---

# Framework Overview

The analytical structure used in this framework is:

```text
Events
↓
Causal Mechanisms
↓
System Archetypes
↓
System Behavior
↓
Strategic Intervention
```

This structure allows analysts to move beyond **surface-level events** and instead understand the **structural drivers behind system behavior**.
